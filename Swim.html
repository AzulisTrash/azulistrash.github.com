Title: Swim

Medium: Oak, Acrylic Paint

Dimensions:

Class: ART13

This small sculpture was my first piece made using the tools provided in the woodshop at SJSU. The concept behind the piece was to take two shapes drawn inspired by two different words to create a sculpture that is completely different shape in the cross cut. The two words that were orignally used for the first two shapes were "flame" and "Slash."
