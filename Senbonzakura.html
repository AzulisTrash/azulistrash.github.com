Title: Senbonzakura

Medium: Adobe Lightroom

Dimensions: 1280x853 pixels

Class: PHOT40.

This photo is part of a series taken at the Japanese Friendship garden. It features a costume that I made for a character named Kagamine Rin in the music video Senbonzakura. My goal with this series was to create a calming, ambient atmosphere and I believe this picture from the set best captures that aesthetic.
