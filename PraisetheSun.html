Title: Parise the Sun

Medium: Wire and Solder

Class: ART68

This work is a small sculpture I made as my first attempt at creating something using wire and solder. The form of the sculpture is supposed to resemble an idol that a person might use while worshiping a god combind with the image of a person themself worshiping the god.
