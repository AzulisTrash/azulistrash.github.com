Title: New Horizons

Medium: PaintTool SAI, Adobe After Effects

Dimensions: 964x936 pixels

Class: ART105

The art style is heavily inspired by that of the Nintendo game Animal Crossing: New Horizons. Since quarantine has started, I've found that Animal Crossing is a game that I come back to for an hour or two every day to maintain my village and talk to all my neighbor villagers. I wanted to visualize the calm and relaxed feeling I have while I'm playing through this animation.
