Title: Broken But Still Beautiful

Medium: Oak, Resin

Dimensions:

Class: ART68

This piece was one of my first projects working with resin. The inspiration behind this piece comes from the Japanese idea of kintsugi or kintsukuroi which is the art of repairing pottery by mending the broken areas with lacqure dusted or mixed with bowdered gold. The piece is meant to be a commentary on how even broken tools can still hold a sense of beauty.
