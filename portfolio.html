About Me

I'm a digital artist that focuses mainly on character design and world map building. I also dabble in animation, watercolor painting, and music composition.I've always been interested in videogames, and my interest led me into character design. I do most of my drawing in PaintToolSai and use programs such as PhotoScape and Photoshop to add small edits to my illustrations.
