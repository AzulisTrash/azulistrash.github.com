Title: Azul (Fullbody Character Design)

Medium: PaintTool SAI

Dimensions: 572x1665 pixels, 515x1666 pixels

These works are two designs for my online persona. It's heavily inspired by many of the different games and shows that I've watched over the years and has evolved many times over the years into the current design presented. I consider it an ever evolving art piece that will always be in progress of being made since I'll most definately continue to change the design as my online image and personal tastes continue to evolve.
